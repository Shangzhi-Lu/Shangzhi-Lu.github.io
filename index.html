About Me

Hi, I'm Shangzhi Lu! You can call me Lubury. I am currently a new PhD student studying Applied Psychology at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.

Before pursuing my PhD degree, I worked for one year at the Academy of Social Sciences in my hometown. Additionally, I earned my bachelor's degree in Psychology from Northwest Normal University and completed my master's degree at CUHK-SZ.

I am interested in Educational Intervention and Meta-science. Recently, I have been focused on the impact of socioeconomic factors on the Critical Thinking of high school students. Besides, I am still working with my master's advisor on a tutorial paper about R data visualization.
